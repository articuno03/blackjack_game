WELCOME TO BLACKJACK                                         
                                                             
OBJECTIVE: REACH 21, BUT DONT GO OVER!                       
                                                             
COMMANDS:                                                    
                                                             
HIT: DRAW CARD                                               
STAND: KEEP CURRENT HAND                                     
                                                             
HOW TO PLAY:                                                 
                                                             
- EACH PLAYER DEALT 2 CARDS                                  
- IF YOUR CARDS EQUAL HIGHER THAN THE SERVERS CARDS, YOU WIN!
- IF YOUR CARDS ARE BELOW THE SERVERS CARDS, YOU LOSE :(     
- IF YOUR CARDS ARE OVER 21, BUST YOU LOSE :(                
                                                             
HAVE FUN!                                                    
                                                             
ADDITIONAL COMMANDS:                                         
                                                             
CHAT: COMMUNICATE WITH PLAYERS                               
QUIT: LEAVE THE GAME
